about this blog

The Office, Parks and Recreation, and Brookyln Nine-Nine are unquestionably some of the best comedies and sitcoms to grace our screens, and this blog is dedicated to honoring them with content that explores their, well, awesomeness. The world is a pretty bleak place right now, and we all need an outlet to relax and just forget about our worries. And why dedicate your time and energy to indulging in a new obsession when you can rewatch and binge these brilliant shows over and over again?

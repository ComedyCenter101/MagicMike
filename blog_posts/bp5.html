The Office of Brookyln

The Office has some of the most notable and memorable cast, and a few of them made a brief cameo in Brookyln Nine-Nine! While not as many as in Parks and Recreation, it's still interesting to see some familiar faces!

Ed Helms:

He plays the lovable but slightly obnoxious Andy Bernard, a salesman, a regional manager, and a notable Cornell alumnus. While Helms plays one of the main characters in The Office, he only makes a brief cameo in Brooklyn Nine-Nine as Jack Danger (pronounced "Donger"), an arrogant agent of the United States Postal Inspection Service. While Andy is relatively harmless (or at least until the last season), Danger/Donger put Jake and Charles at risk with his incompetence, spitefulness, and condescension.

Oscar Nunez:

Nunez plays Oscar Martinez, one of the three accountants on The Office. While his role there is only as a side character, he makes an even smaller cameo than Ed Helms, appearing in Brookyln Nine-Nine for only a few minutes as Dr. Porp, who tells Jake and Captain Holt that they have mumps, in the amazing Season Three episode 9 Days.

Kate Flannery:

Another side character, Flannery plays Meredith Palmer, who's in charge of purchasing and supplier relations but just mostly plays solitaire all day. She too makes a cameo in Brookyln Nine-Nine as Marge Bronigan or "Mean Marge," the scary head of the Facilities Department who righteously deserves her nickname.

Kyle Bornheimer:

Flipping the switch a little, Bornheimer is a little more known for his role on Brookyln Nine-Nine as Teddy Ramos, Amy's ex-boyfriend who has a bad habit of repeatedly proposing to her (including at her wedding and while she was pregnant and he was married). He has a small, blink-and-you'll-miss-it role as one of two ad company reps who helped make the Dunder-Mifflin ad in The Office.

Craig Robinson:

A man notable in both shows, Robinson plays Darryl Philbin in The Office, the warehouse foreman turned the Head of Shipping and Distribution turned Athlead employee who is always just a little bit done with life. On Brookyln Nine-Nine, he's Doug Judy or the infamous Pontiac Bandit, guilty of stealing over 200 Pontiacs (hence the nickname) and one case of dog fraud (sold a Pekingese dog but "t'was a cat"). He's Jake's "nemesis," but we all know that they're practically best friends.

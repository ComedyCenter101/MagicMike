Jacob Peralta: Musical Genius

Jake Peralta is a man of many talents, and his countless weird but fabulous songs are a clear indication of that. From the iconic Backstreet Boys cold open to his several duets with Doug Judy, here are some random scenes in which Andy Samberg really lets his vocals shine.

First and foremost, Andy Samberg is a beautiful and talented human being who, in addition to being a fantastic actor and comedian, is a phenomenal musician. He's in the comedy musical group The Lonely Island and has been nominated and won numerous awards for his work on music videos and Digital Shorts on Saturday Night Live, including "Shy Ronnie" (featuring Rihanna) and the iconic "D*** In A Box" starring Justin Timberlake. So clearly, in addition to his many other otherworldly attributes, Samberg has a unique and brilliant music gift and an amazing set of vocals, and we are blessed to see that talent play out in Brookyln Nine-Nine.

And so, here are some of the countless notable, memorable, and highly amusing scenes in which Jake likes to sing, and he likes to sing a lot (which is perfect because I like to hear him sing, and I like to hear him sing a lot). These scenes are short, very short, but the snippets are beautiful nonetheless.

I Want It That Way

The iconic cold open has its very own separate fanbase and understandably so. From the moment, he says "Backstreet Boys, I’m familiar," Jake really guarantees that the scene will be a memorable one. The way he slowly loses himself into the song, the way that they effortlessly but somewhat awkwardly harmonize, the way that Number Five really killed his part (and the girl's brother); it's just perfection.

Song: I Want It That Way (Backstreet Boys)

What's Up

They're absolutely right; you just cannot sit in karaoke room silently and not sing. The way that Jake and Judy harmonize is amazing, and once again, their facial expressions really make the scene.

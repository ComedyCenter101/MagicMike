In addition, Fumero also notes that they oversexualized Fanny in the remake, forcing Amy, the resident awkward but totally badass nerd into a role that doesn't really fit her. And I would have to agree, especially since the nurse outfit that Fanny dawns in the trailer seems out of character (and kind of made me uncomfortable).

Now, my personal opinion. It's... weird. It is so weird, man. It's difficult to describe exactly what part of the trailer makes me uneasy, other than to just gesture wildly at all of it, so I won't. However, I don't see myself subscribing to a Canadian streaming channel anytime soon nor do I see myself watching an entire show in French (especially considering I have not picked up the language as well as I should've in my two years of high school French), so for now, I guess I, and the other thousands of Brookyln Nine-Nine fans, will have to "settle" (putting that in quotes because the original, in my totally objective opinion, is far superior) for binging the show a dozen more times.

trivia!

The CPR scene in "Stress Relief" actually saved someone's life! With the scene stressing that chest compressions can be done to the beat of "Staying Alive" by Bee Gees, an Office fan from Arizona was able to successfuly perform CPR on a woman he found slumped over her car. She regained consciousness after around minute and was brought to the hospital, where she was safely discharged.
